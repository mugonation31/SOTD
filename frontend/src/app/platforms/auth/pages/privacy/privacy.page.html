1. Introduction

Predict3 ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our football prediction application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using Predict3, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Data Controller

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the data controller is:

Predict3
Email: privacy@predict3.app
Website: https://predict3.app

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

3.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username
  • First and last name
  • Password (encrypted)

3.2 Prediction Data

  • Match predictions (scores you predict)
  • Gameweek selections
  • Joker usage
  • Points earned

3.3 Group Data

  • Group memberships
  • Admin status
  • Leaderboard positions
  • Group settings you configure

3.4 Usage Data

  • Pages viewed
  • Features used
  • Session duration
  • Performance metrics

3.5 Device Information

  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Operating system
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address (anonymized)

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

4.1 Provide the Service

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process your predictions
  • Calculate scores and update leaderboards
  • Enable group participation
  • Display match fixtures and results

4.2 Communication

  • Send deadline reminders (with your consent)
  • Notify you of important updates
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send service announcements

4.3 Improve the Service

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Develop new features
  • Conduct research and analytics

4.4 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Detect and prevent cheating
  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Ensure fair play
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: You agree to create an account and use our service
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide the prediction service you requested
  • Legitimate Interests: We have a legitimate interest in improving our service and preventing fraud
  • Legal Obligation: We may need to retain certain data for legal compliance

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

6.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication hosting (GDPR compliant)
  • Football Data API: Match fixtures and results (no personal data shared)
  • Analytics Providers: Anonymized usage statistics only

6.2 Within Groups

  • Your username and predictions are visible to other members of groups you join
  • Your leaderboard position is visible to group members
  • Group admins can see member lists

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Necessary to provide services
  • Required for legal, tax, or accounting purposes

After account deletion, we retain certain data (anonymized) for up to 90 days for backup purposes, then permanently delete it. Historical leaderboard data may be retained in aggregated, anonymized form.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the EU, you have the following rights:

8.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

8.2 Right to Rectification

You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data through your account settings.

8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

8.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).

8.5 Right to Object

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

8.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time (may limit service functionality).

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@predict3.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following types of cookies:

9.1 Essential Cookies

Required for authentication and core functionality. Cannot be disabled.

9.2 Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how you use the service (anonymized). You can opt-out in settings.

9.3 Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences (e.g., dark mode).

We do NOT use advertising cookies or third-party tracking for marketing purposes.

10. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure hosting with Supabase (SOC 2 Type II certified)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but take all reasonable precautions.

11. Children's Privacy

Predict3 is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@predict3.app, and we will delete it promptly.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Supabase servers, which may be located in different countries. We ensure that any international transfers comply with GDPR through:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Appropriate safeguards

Supabase complies with GDPR and provides data residency options.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify you via email
  • Display a prominent notice in the app

Your continued use of Predict3 after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
